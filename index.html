About the Film

Bober is a unique paper animation film that tells the story of a young beaver's journey through life. Created by Okno Studios, this film combines traditional paper craft with modern animation techniques to create a visually stunning experience.

The film follows Bober, a curious young beaver, as he navigates the challenges of growing up in a rapidly changing environment. Through stunning paper animation, we explore themes of family, friendship, and environmental awareness. The unique paper-craft style brings a tactile, organic feel to the animation that sets it apart from traditional CGI films.

Production began in early 2023 and is set to premiere in late 2024. The film features an original score by renowned composer Sarah Chen and features the voices of several talented actors in both English and Polish versions.

The animation process involves thousands of hand-cut paper pieces, each carefully crafted and photographed frame by frame. Our team of skilled animators works tirelessly to bring Bober's world to life, creating a unique visual style that combines traditional craftsmanship with modern storytelling techniques.

The film's environmental message is particularly relevant today, as it explores themes of habitat preservation and the delicate balance between nature and human development. Through Bober's eyes, we see the beauty of the natural world and the importance of protecting it for future generations.

The project has received support from various environmental organizations and has been recognized for its innovative approach to animation and storytelling. We're proud to bring this unique vision to audiences worldwide.
